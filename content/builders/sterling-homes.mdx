My experience with Sterling Homes was the third and final chapter in my home-buying journey. The property is at **728 Langley Terrace SE, Airdrie, AB T4A 3R1** — the **"Luna" model**, job number **J#158004**. It was a **quick-possession home**.

**Sales & Pre-Possession**

My first contact was **Jason Tang** (lanark@sterlingcalgary.com). The difference from my previous two builder experiences was immediate — no sales pressure, straight answers. The purchase agreement was comprehensive and clear, with every schedule present and the home warranty included. I worked with two lenders (ATB and CIBC); my CIBC mortgage advisor was **Heather Hannah**. Mortgage approval came on **May 21, 2025**.

The home inspection was conducted on **May 28, 2025** by **Marty Keough** of **Nook & Cranny Home Inspections**. The report came back clean — minor cosmetic issues only.

I took possession on **June 2, 2025**.
