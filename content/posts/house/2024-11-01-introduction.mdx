Welcome. I'm Colton English, and this is the story of my journey buying a new home.

For me, buying a house isn't just a financial transaction — it's a 30-year commitment. It's an exchange of a significant portion of my life for a place of comfort, safety, and security. This blog is my way of documenting that journey honestly: the highs and lows, the good builders and the bad ones, the contracts that fell apart and the one that finally worked.

My journey began in late 2024, without a blueprint (pun intended). All I knew was that I wanted a space of my own — one crafted to my needs, one I could look at years from now as a testament to perseverance and hard-won lessons.

In the beginning, my view of home-building was romantic. Picking paint colours, choosing fixtures, imagining how the light would fall in the living room. As I embarked on this path, that rosy vision was replaced by the gritty reality of managing contracts, navigating deposits, chasing down specifications, and learning building codes complex enough to make your head spin.

Yet I wouldn't trade this journey for anything. The bumps along the way — and there were many — eventually helped me navigate the new home building terrain with growing confidence. Today, as I sit in my home, I can see how each hurdle shaped my understanding of this process.

In this blog, I'll share the stages of my journey: selecting the right builder, managing your budget, dealing with contract disputes, and ultimately, taking possession of a newly built home. All with the intent of equipping you — potential new home buyers and builder researchers — with real, lived experience rather than marketing copy.

I hope in reading my story you'll find solace that you're not alone in this daunting, yet ultimately rewarding, undertaking. I hope that with each post, you discover something that helps ease your worries or clear your confusion.

This blog is my journey home. I hope it provides a map for yours.

Sincerely,
Colton English.
