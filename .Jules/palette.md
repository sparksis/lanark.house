## 2025-05-15 - Visual Ratings and Theme Consistency

**Learning:** Visual star ratings (★, ☆) significantly improve the scannability and intuitive understanding of reviews compared to plain text "X out of 5 stars". Furthermore, ensuring that standalone widgets like legal disclaimers use the site's primary action colors ($linkColour, $hoverColour) reinforces brand consistency and helps users quickly identify interactive elements.

**Action:** Use a reusable star-rating component for all review-based content. Prioritize global SASS variables over hardcoded or framework-default colors for consistency.
