My new home's fireplace, once a focal point of frustration due to missing decorative crystals and a complete failure to turn on, has finally been addressed.

### October 2025: Full Replacement and Resolution

Following the approval by Jaspreet Gill in September 2025, the entire fireplace unit was replaced by Classic Fireplace. I have verified that the replacement unit is fully functional, complete with the decorative crystals that were missing from the original install. This item is now officially marked as **Done** on my tracking board.

While it took several months and multiple service visits to reach this point, I am pleased to have a working fireplace for the upcoming winter season.

**Next Steps:** No further action required for this item. I will simply perform a final visual check during the year-end walkthrough to ensure no issues have developed.
