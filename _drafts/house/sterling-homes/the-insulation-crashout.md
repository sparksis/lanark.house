A definitive remediation plan for the siding, the water system, and the immediate vacuum-removal of the insulation is expected by the end of business today. Failing that, the file moves to the Alberta New Home Warranty Program and the City of Airdrie inspectors.

This isn't just about a house anymore; it's about a systematic refusal to follow through on professional obligations. The wind is burying the fiberglass, but it won't bury the record of this failure.
